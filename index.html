Center for Applied Research in Information Technology

About

Welcome to Kennesaw State University's Center for Applied Research in Information Technology (CARIT) website. This CARIT is home to many different types of researches that are done around KSU. A few things you can find in the CARIT include: outstanding survey research, detailed experimental research, various program evaluations, and performance measurements. Also, the CARIT provides the faculty conducting research with grants, research projects, and much more. Not only is the faculty provided with these things to use but also community organizations and local businesses are also given these perks to the CARIT.

We have created this CARIT website in hopes that everyone across KSU and its community can utilized this site to get news on various different researchers, resources to help start any new researches, etc. We beileve this website will be helpful and hope everyone is able to enjoy and learn about different researches.
