Summer Student Assistant Positions

CARIT provides various opportunities for the students here at Kennesaw State University. There are part time and full time positions avaliable that can help the students get the experience they need to succeed in the future. Each position will teach them different set of skills that will be needed in thier future endeavors. The students get to learn skills such as team work, leadership, communication, honest, responsibility, and many more. These opportunities also give the students a chance to learn from the best in thier particular field. There are many things that are not though in school but with the help of these positions, students get to explore and learn in depth about the real world job experience. Here at CARIT our goal is to help our students to the best of our ability.
